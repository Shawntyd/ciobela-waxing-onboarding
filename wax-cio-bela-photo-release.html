Photography Release

This release defines how Cio Bela may use photos and videos of your work, your likeness, and content created at our location — and how you may use ours.

About This Document

As a contractor at Cio Bela, your work may be photographed, featured, and shared across our marketing channels — including Instagram, TikTok, our website, and promotional materials. This release clarifies exactly what you are and are not consenting to, and what rights you retain over your own content.

This is a two-way agreement. It also outlines your rights to share content captured at Cio Bela on your own platforms, and the guidelines that apply.

Read each section carefully and check only the permissions you are comfortable granting. You may choose to grant all, some, or none of the optional permissions below.

Contractor Information
Your personal or professional account — how Cio Bela will tag you in shared content.
Part 1 — What Cio Bela May Use
These permissions are for Cio Bela's use of content featuring your work or likeness. Check each box you agree to. Unchecked items are not granted. All checked items allow Cio Bela to use your content for the stated purpose with attribution to you where applicable.
Photos & Videos of My Nail Work
Cio Bela may photograph and/or video record completed nail sets at my station and share them on Cio Bela's social media, website, and marketing materials. My artist name or handle will be tagged or credited where possible.
Behind-the-Scenes / Process Content
Cio Bela may capture and share behind-the-scenes content of me working at my station — including process videos, time-lapses, or candid salon footage — for use on social media and marketing platforms.
Professional Headshot / Profile Use
Cio Bela may use my professional headshot or a salon-taken photo of me for my artist profile on the Cio Bela website, booking platform, promotional flyers, or similar materials.
Reposting My Personal Content
Cio Bela may re-share or repost content I publish on my personal social media accounts (Instagram, TikTok, etc.) to Cio Bela's official accounts, provided I am tagged or credited in the original post.
Paid Advertising & Promotions
Cio Bela may use photos or videos of my work or likeness in paid advertising, boosted posts, or promotional campaigns (including Meta Ads, Google Ads, or print).
Press, Media & PR Coverage
Cio Bela may include photos of my work or my name in press releases, media features, editorial coverage, or public relations materials promoting the salon.
Part 2 — Your Rights to Share Cio Bela Content
This section defines what you are permitted to share from Cio Bela on your own platforms. These rights are granted to you by Cio Bela as part of this mutual release.
Portfolio Content of Your Work ✓ Always Permitted
You may always share photos and videos of your completed nail work performed at Cio Bela on your personal and professional social media accounts — including after contract termination. Your artistic portfolio belongs to you.
Salon Aesthetic & Ambiance ✓ Always Permitted
You may share photos that capture the Cio Bela space, ambiance, or aesthetic while you are an active contractor. This includes station setups, product flat-lays, and behind-the-scenes content you capture yourself.
Part 2B — Your Agreed Obligations
The following obligations are agreed to by all contractors and are non-negotiable. They reflect the terms already outlined in your Contractor Agreement.
Client Consent Before Posting *
I will obtain explicit verbal or written consent from any client before posting photos or videos of them — including photos of just their hands — to any public platform. I understand that posting without consent is a violation of my Contractor Agreement and client privacy standards.
Brand Removal Upon Termination *
Upon resignation or termination of my Contractor Agreement, I will remove all references to "Cio Bela" from my social media bios, highlights, and profile descriptions within 24 hours. I understand that failure to do so constitutes Brand Infringement under my Contractor Agreement.
No Negative Brand Content *
I agree not to post content — including photos, videos, captions, stories, or comments — that portrays Cio Bela, its management, staff, or other contractors in a negative, defamatory, or damaging light on any public platform.
Signature & Date
⚠ Required checkboxes above: You must check all three obligation boxes in Part 2B (client consent, brand removal, and no negative content) before this release can be finalized. These obligations are non-negotiable per your Contractor Agreement.
