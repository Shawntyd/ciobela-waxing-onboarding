Section 4 — Most Important

Taxpayer Identification Number

Provide either your Social Security Number (SSN) or Employer Identification Number (EIN) — not both.

SSN vs. EIN — which do I use?

• SSN — Use this if you're an individual, sole proprietor, or single-member LLC filing under your own name. Most common for independent contractors.

• EIN — Use this if you have a registered business entity with its own IRS-issued EIN.
